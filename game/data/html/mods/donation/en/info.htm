Your purchases can be processed by Mercado Pago or PayPal, and must be paid within the payment deadline, otherwise they will be cancelled.
When choosing to pay with PIX, you can pay through the QR Code displayed right after purchasing your item, or through the "copy and paste" code sent to your email.
If you chose another payment method, we will send you an email with the link where the order should be completed. You can also generate a QR Code to access it on your phone and pay through the app or browser.
After making the payment, click the "I Paid" button on the order status screen. If everything is correct, your items will be delivered immediately.
